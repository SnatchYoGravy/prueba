Arturo Garcia Cardona

Autobiography: A Life Between Two Worlds I was born in Guadalajara, Jalisco, a city known for its vibrant culture, mariachi music, and rich history. My early years were rooted in Mexico, but my life took a significant turn at the age of five when my mom and I moved to Hot Springs, Arkansas, in the United States. This small town, nestled in the Ouachita Mountains, became my second home for the next 13 years. Growing up between two countries and cultures shaped who I am today, giving me a unique perspective on life and a deep appreciation for both my Mexican heritage and American upbringing.
